127.	The SECRETARY-GENERAL:
I just wish to inform the Council that at this moment a report from my representative, Mr. Mayobre, is on the way; the indications are that it is going to be an important report.
128.	I was wondering whether the Council would agree to a suspension of the meeting for a half hour or so. I expect to be able to submit this report in about a half hour. Of course, if it is the desire of the Council, you may wish to consider the advisability of having an afternoon meeting at 2.30 or 3 o'clock.
