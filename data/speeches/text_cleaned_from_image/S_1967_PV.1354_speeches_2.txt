89.	The SECRETARY-GENERAL:
In response to the request of the representative of the Soviet Union, I shall read out that portion of my report. In the course of the Telex conversation between General Odd Bull and Undersecretary Ralph Bunche at the time of my earlier report, we received this information: Report from the Chairman of Israel-Syrian Mixed Armistice Commission. 10 June. Dis-
patch at 0735 hours GMT: “An air attack took place on the Damascus airport area.”
90.	I have just received a further report on the same subject from General Bull. It reads:
“Most immediate from Bull, Report received from Chairman of the Israel-Syrian Mixed Armistice Commission. Damascus at 0923 hours GMT, air attack on Damascus going on.”
